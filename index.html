Marketing 101: Understanding the Basics

All online marketing campaigns for a company, item, or service are considered to be part of digital marketing. It uses strategies including social media marketing, email marketing, content marketing, and search engine optimisation. Digital marketing enables companies to connect with and interact with their target market, increasing brand recognition and fostering company expansion.

A seamless consumer experience across many channels and devices is the goal of omnichannel marketing. To give customers a consistent and tailored experience, it involves integrating online and offline connections including social media, email, websites, and physical stores. Businesses may strengthen their ties with clients and boost brand loyalty by offering a unified experience.
